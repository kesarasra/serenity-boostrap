Our Vision

To become a trusted and preferred place of choice where those who need mental health, trauma victims, and their families can rely on receiving high-quality psychological well-being services in Myanmar.

Our Values

  • Trust
  • Respect
  • Competency
  • Confidentiality
  • Compassion

Our Mission

  • To provide quality mental health service to people all over Myanmar.
  • To create, promote and maintain a positive relationship with our clients.
  • To improve the role of qualified lay counselors who have been trained to provide ethical mental health services
