Our Vision

To become a trusted and preferred place of choice where those who need mental health, trauma victims, and their families can rely on receiving high-quality psychological well-being services in Myanmar.

Our Mission

To provide quality mental health services across Myanmar and foster positive relationships with our clients.

To support and develop trained lay counselors in delivering ethical mental health care.
