About

Ms. Agnes Mary is a Psychotherapist & Counseling Psychologist, a founder of Serenity counseling and psychological services. She holds a Master of Science in Counseling Psychology from Assumption University, Thailand. She is also a trauma-focused EMDR therapist. Having the passion to work for people and to understand the people who she is working with, occasionally she attended the training related to the field of psychology and remarkably in 2009, she joined the Diploma in Applied Psychology at the University of Yangon. She has recently completed ‘Mental Health in Complex Emergencies’ course provided by Fordham University, New York. Currently, she is studying Ph.D. Program in Women’s Gender and Sexuality Program at Chiang Mai University, Thailand. In addition, she worked with UNFPA Myanmar as part of MHPSS (Mental Health and Psychosocial Support) Roster Team. She has experience in working with the refugees and migrant workers from different countries. She worked with the Issara Institute as community outreach and empowerment officer for the Myanmar migrant workers across Thailand. Also, she served as a workplace counselor at TDCX, Thailand. Basically, she provides individual counseling to adolescents and adults from various background and age group. She takes integrative approach to her therapeutic practice from different psychological approaches including Cognitive Behavior Therapy (CBT), Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing (EMDR) and Mindfulness.
