About

Ms. Naw Aye Thiri Myint @ Grace is a Counseling Psychologist and holds a Master of Science in Counseling Psychology from Assumption University, Thailand. She is currently working as an Assistant Lecturer in the Social Studies Department of the Myanmar Institute of Theology (Liberal Arts Program). She has experience in school (or) guidance counseling and works with teaching and counseling. She worked as a guidance counseling intern at Pan-Asia International School in Bangkok, Thailand from 2016-2017. She is currently teaching Social Studies and Social Psychology to students majoring in Social Psychology and Personality Psychology at the Myanmar Institute of Theology, as well as guiding students to improve their self-esteem, coping with stress and anxiety while studying.
