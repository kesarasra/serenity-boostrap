Activities

Suicide Prevention Sharing Circle

Runs every Tuesday at 8PM (MM Time) via ZOOM, and opens free for everyone This session provides a safe and empathetic space for individuals who are struggling with suicidal thoughts, as well as for those who want to offer mental health support and understanding. Led by trained lay counselors, these sessions focus on fostering connection, promoting emotional well-being, and providing resources to help prevent suicide.

Serenity Supervision Sessions

Runs on Mondays (twice a month) at 7:00 - 8:30 PM (MM Time), and opens free for Serenity Alumni. 1. Skill Development: Discussing cases with a supervisor as well as getting constructive feedback to allow practicing case workers and case managers to gain insight into their own performance and skills. 2. Self-care and Emotional Support: Supervision sessions serve as a safe space where case workers and case managers can openly discuss challenging cases and personal reactions, helping professionals manage their own self-care and overall well-being. 3. Ethical Guidance: Supervisors help professionals navigate complex ethical dilemmas and ensure that they adhere to ethical standards and guidelines in their own practice. This is especially important in the mental health field, where client well-being is of great importance.

Emotional Support Session (Fridays)

Runs every Friday at 8 PM (MM Time) via ZOOM and is one of our free session for each and everyone. The primary goal of Serenity emotional sessions is to provide emotional support, guidance, and a safe space for the individuals to express and explore their feelings, thoughts, and concerns. Overall, Serenity emotional support sessions provide a valuable opportunity for each and every one to process their emotions, gain perspective, and develop skills to better manage their psychological well-being.
